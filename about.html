Our History

  • Our Experience

  • The Founders Meet

    It all began in the heart of a bustling city, where our founders met over a shared love for gastronomy and a vision for a culinary revolution.

  • Create A Restaurant

    Our first restaurant was born in a quaint neighborhood, setting the stage for a new era of fine dining experiences that soon captivated locals and visitors alike.

  • Added 200+ Employees

    We rapidly grew our team, hiring over 200 passionate and talented individuals who brought creativity, dedication, and a shared vision to our thriving brand.


  • More Restaurants Outlet

  • Established Restaurant in Europe

    Our European expansion marked a significant milestone, where we established a strong presence and brought our unique flavors to new, discerning audiences.

  • Franchise Restaurants Brooklyn

    Our entry into the Brooklyn market was met with overwhelming enthusiasm, where we launched several franchise locations, offering an authentic taste of excellence.

  • Added 100K More Employees

    Driven by rapid growth, we expanded our team with an additional 100,000 employees, each playing a pivotal role in achieving our ambitious global goals.

  • Established Marketing

    Our comprehensive marketing strategy was pivotal in amplifying our brand, reaching audiences across diverse platforms, and enhancing our global presence.
